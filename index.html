Our Story

For over two decades, broast furooj sanabil has been synonymous with exceptional hospitality and mouth-watering cuisine. What started as a single location has grown into a beloved chain of 7 branches, each maintaining our commitment to quality and service.

Our Specialty

We're renowned for our signature broasted chicken - perfectly seasoned, crispy on the outside, and tender on the inside. Our menu also features gourmet burgers, fresh salads, and comfort food that keeps families coming back for more.
